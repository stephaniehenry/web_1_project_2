Stephanie Henry, Portraits

Stephanie Henry

shenry11@eagles.ewu.edu

Portraits

I took these photos for the portrait unit in Digital Photography, winter quarter 2019.

The first two portraits were taken with three light sources. The second two were taken with one light.

After the photos were taken, I took several hours to go through all of them and choose my nine favorites to edit. The editing process included color correcting, paining out lights and bounce cards, teeth whitening, and facial touch-ups.

The toned images were done in duotone.
